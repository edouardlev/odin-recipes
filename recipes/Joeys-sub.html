Joey's Meatball Sub

back

it's apparently "the greatest sandwich in the world."

Before the whole gang got together I prepped Joey's Marinara Sauce, which is also from the book. Loaded with garlic and tons of fresh herbs like parsley, oregano, thyme, and basil, my apartment smelled like an Italian restaurant within an hour. *Chef's kiss*

Ingredients

  • Your favorite sub bread
  • 7 frozen meatballs
  • 1/4 cup marinara sauce
  • New york steak spice
  • 1/4 cup parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Cut your bread in half, lengthwise
  2. Airfry your meatballs at 400 for 8 minutes
  3. place meatballs on bread, sauce on top, add your spice , sprinkle cheese
  4. Airfry again to toast, for 5 minutes at 375
  5. let cool and bon appetit!
